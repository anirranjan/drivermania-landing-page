DriverMania

An Open World Driving Game made with Unity!

About

DriverMania is a 3D Driving Game where players can drive fantastic cars in a diverse open world environment. Join the drivers exploring the various places to go in Driver City or setting a new high score in the soccer mode!

Here's what one of DriverMania's players has to say about the game: "It's a really good arcade racer. The car designs are familar yet unique and soccer mode is a blast."

Game Features

6 Awesome Cars

There are six cars to choose from, each with its unique style.

2 Game Modes

Players can choose between Driving Mode and Soccer Mode.

Easy to Play

The game has intuitive and straightforward controls.

Free to Download

The game is free to play, and has no in-app purchases.
